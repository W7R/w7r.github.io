A Microsoft Flight Simulator Module

Experience flying one of World War II's most iconic medium bombers, the B-25J. Currently in development by Welds & Rivets for Microsoft Flight Simulator.

This project aims to capture the spirit of the classic warbird with meticulous attention to historical accuracy. From the roar of the twin Wright R-2600 engines, to an intricately modeled cockpit with authentic instruments. Every detail is being crafted using original blueprints from North American Aviation to immerse pilots in the era. Once complete, it will offer a breathtaking opportunity to experience the power, precision, and legacy of the legendary B-25J in stunning virtual skies.

Welds & Rivets is a team of aviation enthusiasts. Bringing our decades of experience in development and artistry together to create projects we are passionate about and want to experience ourselves; we hope you enjoy it as much as we will.
